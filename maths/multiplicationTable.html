    Tips
    • If you forget a ×9, do a ×10 and substract. E.g.: 8×9 = (8×10) - 8 = 80 - 8 = 72.
    • ×5 is half of ×10. E.g.: 4×8 = 80 ÷ 2 = 40
    • Remembering the ×5's can help you to get the ×4 and ×6 by subtracting and adding respectively.
      E.g.: 4×8 = (40) - 8 = 32
